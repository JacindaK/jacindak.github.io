Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are a pretty eggciting dish and are also in a way kinda weird and wet.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has been melted, add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist, remove them from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
