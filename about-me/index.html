~ About Me ~

Hello! I'm Jacinda, known as Litten and Upsetty Spaghetti online, and I am 18 years old as a senior at Craig High School. I work every other weekend as a checker/cashier at Woodman's and volunteer for the Humane Society at PetCo to play with and help take care of adoptable cats there. In my free time, I enjoy playing various games, like Minecraft and Phasmophobia, but typically play Valorant the most. I enjoy playing these games with my friends I know in person, my boyfriend, and even some friends I've met online through these games. While I am a gamer, I also am very passionate about art. I took up a paid internship for graphic design this summer and love drawing when I can. I plan to go to Whitewater to major in graphic design as a future career. In the meantime, I continue to build up my portfolio via my public art account on Instagram and attempt to get myself out there by streaming on twitch.
